adsorbable organic halidesni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: https://saref.etsi.org/saref4watr/AdsorbableOrganicHalides

belongs to
chemical property c
has facts
has description dp " AOX stands for Adsorbable Organic Halides, it is a measure of the halogenated organic compounds present in a water or waste stream.
